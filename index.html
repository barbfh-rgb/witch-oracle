The Witch's Oracle
Your prompt tool for reflection, journaling, meditation and quiet thought.
Draw one prompt when you want something to ponder, journal with, meditate on or carry through your day.
You do not have to answer it perfectly. You do not have to answer it all at once. Let the prompt be a small doorway. You might write about it, sit with it beside a candle, use it as a spell seed, take it for a walk or simply notice what thoughts and feelings arise.
Some prompts are light and curious. Some may lead into shadow work, memory or healing. Take what feels useful. Leave what does not. Return when you are ready.
